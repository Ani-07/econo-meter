About Econo-meter


Econo-meter measures public sentiment about the Economy of the United States of America based on sentiment of tweets tweeted regarding the Economy in the past 24 hours. The Econo-meter score is updated every 3 hours based on fresh tweets and removing any tweets older than 24 hours

To learn more about our process of arriving at the Econo-meter, visit the Methodology section To view historical Econo-meter scores visit the Historical Scores section
